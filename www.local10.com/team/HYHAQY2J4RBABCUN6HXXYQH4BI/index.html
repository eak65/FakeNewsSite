Roy Ramos

Reporter

Roy Ramos joined the Local 10 News team in 2018.

Roy is a South Florida native who grew up in Florida City. He attended Christopher Columbus High School, Homestead Senior High School and graduated from St. Thomas University.

Roy started his career in news at WSVN, where he worked as a general assignment reporter, but he has worked in several different markets in Florida, most recently at WFTV in Orlando.

He has covered a range of stories from the BP oil spill, countless hurricanes, and the 2016 president campaign and election to the Pulse Nightclub tragedy, for which he was nominated for an Emmy Award.
