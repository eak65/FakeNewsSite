Ian Margol

Reporter

Ian Margol joined the Local 10 News team in July 2016 as a general assignment reporter.

Born in Miami Beach and raised in Broward County, Ian is thrilled to be back home in South Florida. 

After graduating from Cooper City High in 2009, he moved on to Emory University in Atlanta, where he double majored in journalism and sociology.

Before starting at WPLG, Ian reported and anchored at KKCO/KJCT in Grand Junction, Colorado, from 2013 to 2014, and then WSAV in Savannah, Georgia, from 2014 to 2016. He was also an intern and, eventually, a freelance associate producer for the CBS News program "48 Hours" for several years in college.

A huge South Florida sports fan, when he isn't at work, you can find Ian at Miami Hurricanes and Dolphins football games and Florida Panthers hockey games.
