Here are the number of coronavirus cases in individual cities in Miami-Dade and Broward County, Florida, from data verified as of Tuesday, June 30 from Florida Department of Health. (County list numbers released earlier than city by city.) The information below includes the cities in Broward County and Miami-Dade County for cases of Florida residents based on zip code of person tested. Numbers are from results received by the Florida Department of Health from the state public health laboratory (BPHL) as well as commercial and hospital labs. No change in number or low number does not mean no new cases of COVID-19; reports may be pending.
